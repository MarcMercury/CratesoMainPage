Data Deletion Request

Your data, your choice

Your Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. We are committed to your privacy and data sovereignty.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open CHO and go to Settings
  2. Select "Account"
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@crateso.com with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - CHO"
  • Include: Your account email address

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile and preferences
  • All product and inventory records
  • Warranty and maintenance tracking data
  • Subscription records and history
  • Vendor and contractor logs
  • Uploaded documents and receipts
  • Connected service tokens and data

What We May Retain

  • Transaction records for compliance (typically 7 years)
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage data
  • Information required by law

Processing Time

  • In-App: Immediate deactivation, full deletion within 30 days
  • Email: Response within 5 business days, deletion within 30 days
  • Backups: Up to 90 days for complete removal from all systems

Before You Delete

  • Export First: Download your household data before deletion
  • Irreversible: Deleted data cannot be recovered
  • Shared Households: Other members will lose access to shared data
  • Subscriptions: Cancel any CHO premium subscription first
