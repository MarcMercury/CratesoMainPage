Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CHO ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our Chief Household Officer Dashboard. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account registration details (name, email)
  • Household information and preferences
  • Product and appliance information (barcodes, purchase dates, warranties)
  • Vendor and contractor records
  • Documents you upload (receipts, manuals, contracts)

Information from Connected Services

  • Email data (receipts and purchase confirmations, with your consent)
  • Bank transaction data (subscription detection, with your consent)
  • Cloud storage documents (with your consent)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information and identifiers
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions
  • App performance and error logs

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize the CHO experience
  • Track warranties and send maintenance reminders
  • Detect and manage subscriptions
  • Store and organize vendor and contractor history
  • Generate household reports and insights
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate important updates and alerts

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive financial data
  • Secure, encrypted storage for all documents
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating CHO (under strict confidentiality)
  • Third-party APIs for product information lookup (anonymized)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Export all your household data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect any linked services
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

CHO is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
