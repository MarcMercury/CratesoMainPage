Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product to my inventory?

Open the CHO app, tap the "+" button, and either scan the product barcode or manually enter the details. CHO will automatically fetch available product information, manuals, and warranty details.

How does subscription detection work?

With your permission, CHO connects to your bank account (read-only) to identify recurring charges. You can review detected subscriptions and take action directly from the app.

Can I share my household with my partner?

Yes! CHO supports household sharing. Invite your partner via email, and they'll have full access to the shared household dashboard. Both users can add, edit, and manage household items.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your household data at any time. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 30 days. See our Data Deletion page for details.

Troubleshooting

Barcode not scanning?

Ensure good lighting and hold the camera steady. If a barcode isn't in our database, you can manually enter the product details.

Connected service not syncing?

Try disconnecting and reconnecting the service in Settings. If issues persist, contact support with the error message.
