Contact Us

For questions, feedback, or assistance with SLAPP, please reach out to us:

  • Email: info@crateso.com
  • Subject Line: Please include "SLAPP Support" in your subject
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 24-48 business hours

Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you faster, please include:

  • Your phone number associated with your account
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version (found in Settings)
  • A detailed description of your issue or question
  • Screenshots if applicable
