A Story Operating System

Most tools help you write. SLAPP helps you build stories. Instead of separating writing, planning, visuals, and structure into different tools, SLAPP connects everything into a single, living system.

Characters evolve as you write them. Scenes understand what they depend on. Visual storyboards update narrative structure. Rearranging moments reshapes the story intelligently. Every element is aware of every other element.

This is not a document editor. This is a story intelligence engine.

Key Concepts

📦 Story Objects

Everything in SLAPP is an object: Character, Scene, Place, Item, Event, Relationship, Idea. These objects connect to each other, update over time, and drive story intelligence.

📖 Living Bios

Characters and elements evolve automatically based on story usage. The system tracks where they've been, what they know, how they've changed, and who they affect.

👁️ Continuity Awareness

SLAPP actively monitors contradictions, missing setups, broken causality, and dropped story threads — surfacing them without interrupting your flow.

🔒 Canon Lock

Lock key truths in your world. "This character never lies." "Magic cannot revive the dead." The system will respect these constraints throughout your story.
