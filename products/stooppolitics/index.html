Politics doesn't have to be toxic to be real.

Stoop Politics is built around the idea that most political change happens locally, most people care about their immediate community, and most of what divides us nationally doesn't actually apply to whether we should repave the street or add more benches to the park.

We built tools for that conversation — not the screaming match.
