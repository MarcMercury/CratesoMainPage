Token System

Understanding the Chaos Economy

  • Token Assignment: Tokens are randomly assigned when you visit. There's no purchase option.
  • Token Value: Tokens have zero monetary value. You cannot cash out.
  • Creator Earnings: Creators get 90% of tokens spent on their content.
  • The House: We keep 10% as a containment fee.

Common Token Questions

  • Why is my balance random? That's the point. Chaos economics.
  • Can I buy more tokens? No. Tokens cannot be purchased.
  • Can I transfer tokens? No. Use them or lose them.
  • Where do tokens go when I unlock content? 90% to the creator, 10% to the house. Check the House Tally.
