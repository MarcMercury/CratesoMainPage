Data Deletion Request

Your data, your choice

Your Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. We are committed to your privacy.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open TAG and go to Settings
  2. Select "Account"
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@crateso.com with:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - TAG"
  • Include: Your account email address

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile and preferences
  • Age verification records
  • Game preferences and saved configurations
  • Usage history and analytics
  • Any community interactions

What We May Retain

  • Transaction records for compliance (typically 7 years)
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage data
  • Information required by law
  • Fraud prevention records

Processing Time

  • In-App: Immediate deactivation, full deletion within 30 days
  • Email: Response within 5 business days, deletion within 30 days
  • Backups: Up to 90 days for complete removal

Before You Delete

  • Irreversible: Deleted data cannot be recovered
  • Re-verification Required: Creating a new account will require fresh age verification
  • Subscriptions: Cancel active subscriptions to avoid charges
