Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to TAG! This Privacy Policy explains how Crateso ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use TAG to play social games with friends and family. We believe gaming should be fun, social, and safe.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create a TAG account, we collect:

  • Display name or username
  • Email address (for account recovery)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)

1.2 Game Activity

  • Games played and scores
  • Win/loss records
  • Game preferences and favorites
  • Achievements and badges earned
  • Time spent playing

1.3 Social Data

  • Friend connections
  • Game room participation
  • Chat messages (in game rooms)
  • Voice chat (not recorded, real-time only)

1.4 Technical Information

  • Device type and browser
  • IP address and general location
  • App performance data
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Gameplay: Match you with players, save progress, display leaderboards
  • Social Features: Connect you with friends, enable game rooms
  • Improvements: Analyze which games are popular, fix bugs
  • Safety: Prevent cheating and abusive behavior
  • Communications: Send game invites and important updates

3. Sharing Information

What's Visible to Other Players

  • Your display name and profile picture
  • Game scores and achievements
  • Online status (can be hidden in settings)

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, support
  • Anti-Cheat Partners: To maintain fair play
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

4. Children's Privacy

TAG is designed for players 13 and older. If we discover a user is under 13 without parental consent, we will delete their account. Parents can contact parents@crateso.com with questions.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption for all data transfers
  • Secure, hashed password storage
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited employee access

6. Your Choices

  • Privacy Settings: Control who sees your activity
  • Notifications: Manage email and push preferences
  • Data Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete Account: Remove your account and data
  • Friend Management: Block or unfriend players

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app usage

You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while you use TAG
  • Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  • Chat logs: Retained for 90 days for safety
  • Anonymized analytics: May be retained indefinitely

9. International Users

TAG is operated from the United States. By using TAG, you consent to data transfer to the US with appropriate privacy protections.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? We're here to help:

  • Email: privacy@crateso.com
  • In-app: Settings → Privacy → Contact
