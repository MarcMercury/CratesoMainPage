Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Our Privacy-First Philosophy

CLO (Chief Life Officer) is built on a "Local-First" philosophy. We believe your personal data—your thoughts, relationships, and home information—belongs to you and only you.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and basic profile information necessary to provide the service.

Your Content

CLO stores your personal data including:

  • Self module: Journal entries, tasks, health data integrations, and personal notes
  • Relationships module: Shared capsules, communication preferences, and vault contents
  • Home module: Inventory, warranties, maintenance schedules, and household logistics

All personal content is encrypted with bank-grade encryption and stored locally on your device whenever possible.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the CLO service
  • Enable cross-module "Fluid Intelligence" features you've enabled
  • Sync data across your devices (encrypted end-to-end)
  • Send service-related communications

What We Never Do

  • We never sell your data
  • We never show you ads
  • We never share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We never train AI models on your personal content
  • We never access your Vault data—it's encrypted with keys only you and your partner hold

Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all personal data
  • Zero-knowledge architecture for Vault contents
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure, encrypted data transmission

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data at any time
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Control what data syncs to the cloud vs. stays local

Contact Us
