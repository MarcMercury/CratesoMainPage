Frequently Asked Questions

What is CLO?

CLO (Chief Life Officer) is a unified life management platform that helps you organize three core areas: your Self (personal wellness and tasks), your Relationships (connection with important people), and your Home (household management).

Is my data private?

Absolutely. CLO is built on a "Local-First" philosophy with bank-grade encryption. We never sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with third parties.

How does the Vault work?

The Vault is a double-locked storage feature in the Relationships module. Both you and your partner must agree to access Vault contents—neither party can access it alone.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export all your data at any time in standard formats. Your data belongs to you.

What platforms is CLO available on?

CLO is coming soon to iOS and Android. Join our waitlist to be notified when we launch.
