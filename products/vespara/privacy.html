Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Vespara! This Privacy Policy explains how Crateso ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Vespara, our Relationship Management System (RMS) and Consent-Forward Game Engine. We take your privacy seriously, especially given the intimate nature of our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account & Profile Information

When you create a Vespara account and complete The Interview, we collect:

  • Display name and username
  • Email address and phone number (for verification)
  • Profile photos
  • Gender, pronouns, and sexual orientation
  • Relationship status and availability
  • Heat level preferences and hard limits
  • Location (city-level for matching)
  • Your 140-character "Hook" and AI-generated bio

1.2 Relationship & Connection Data

  • Match history and connection pipeline stages
  • Conversation momentum scores
  • Vouch chain verifications
  • Group/Circle memberships
  • Event participation history

1.3 Game Activity (TAGS)

  • Games played and consent levels selected
  • Achievement progress
  • Session history (anonymized)

1.4 AI Interaction Data

  • Strategist conversation logs
  • Ghost Protocol usage (drafts, not sent messages)
  • AI-generated insights and nudges

1.5 Technical Information

  • Device type, OS, and browser
  • IP address and general location
  • App performance and error logs
  • Tonight Mode location check-ins (opt-in only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Matching: Connect you with compatible people based on preferences, heat levels, and logistics
  • The Hub: Power all 8 modules of your social operating system
  • AI Features: Provide Strategist advice, Ghost Protocol drafts, and Resuscitator scripts
  • TAGS Games: Enable consent-forward gameplay with appropriate partners
  • Safety: Verify identities, prevent harassment, enforce community standards
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform (anonymized data only)

3. Sharing Information

What's Visible to Other Users

  • Your profile (photos, bio, hook, heat level)
  • Online status and Tonight Mode location (if enabled)
  • Vouch chain verification status
  • Mutual connection indicators

What's NEVER Shared

  • Your hard limits (private to you only)
  • Shredder/Ghost Protocol history
  • AI conversation logs
  • Exact location data

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share with:

  • Supabase (secure cloud infrastructure)
  • OpenAI (for AI features, no personal identifiers sent)
  • Law enforcement (only when legally required)

4. Data Security

Given the sensitive nature of Vespara, we implement industry-leading security:

  • End-to-end encryption for all messages
  • Row-level security on all database tables
  • Regular security audits
  • Encrypted storage of all personal data
  • Two-factor authentication available

5. Your Rights & Controls

  • Access: View all data we have about you in the Mirror → Settings tab
  • Correction: Update your profile and preferences anytime
  • Deletion: Request complete data deletion via Data Deletion
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Pause: Temporarily hide your profile without losing data

6. Age Requirement

Vespara is strictly for adults 21 years of age or older. We verify age during The Interview (Clearance phase). Accounts found to be underage will be immediately terminated.

7. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@vespara.co
  • Support: Vespara Support

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Vespara evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through the app and email.
