Best Practices

Guidelines for Intentional Connection

The Vespara Philosophy

Vespara exists to replace match burnout with meaningful connection. These best practices will help you get the most out of your experience while keeping yourself and others safe.

1. The Interview: Be Authentic

The 7-phase Interview is your foundation. Here's how to approach it:

Heat Level Selection

  • 🌸 Mild: Choose this if you prioritize emotional connection first
  • 🔥 Medium: A balanced approach — open but not rushing
  • 🌶️ Hot: You know what you want and aren't shy about it
  • ☢️ Nuclear: For the experienced and adventurous

Be honest — mismatched heat levels lead to frustration for everyone.

Hard Limits

Take this seriously. Your hard limits are used to:

  • Filter out incompatible matches before they see your profile
  • Ensure TAGS games respect your boundaries
  • Protect you without requiring awkward conversations

The Hook (140 Characters)

This is your first impression. Make it count:

  • Be specific, not generic
  • Show personality, not just preferences
  • Avoid clichés ("looking for my partner in crime")

2. Using The Hub Effectively

🪞 MIRROR (Profile & Analytics)

  • Check the TRUTH tab regularly — AI insights can reveal patterns
  • Update your BUILD tab as your interests evolve
  • Review settings periodically in the SETTINGS tab

🔍 DISCOVER (Swipe Marketplace)

  • Read profiles fully — we've optimized for depth, not speed
  • Use the "Save for Later" feature when unsure
  • Quality over quantity — intentional swipes matter

🏠 NEST (Connection CRM)

  • Use pipeline stages to track where connections stand
  • Add notes after conversations to remember details
  • Regularly review and clean up stale connections

💬 WIRE (Messaging)

  • Respond within 48 hours to maintain momentum
  • Voice messages build connection faster than text
  • If a conversation feels dead, try the Resuscitator

📅 PLANNER (AI Calendar)

  • Let AI suggest optimal times based on both schedules
  • Don't overbook — quality time matters
  • Use the buffer feature between dates

👥 GROUP (Events & Parties)

  • Host events to build your circle
  • Attend events to meet people in low-pressure settings
  • Respect privacy — what happens at events stays at events

👻 SHREDDER (Ghost Protocol)

  • Use it — ghosting without closure hurts people
  • Review AI drafts and personalize them
  • Be kind, be honest, be brief

🎲 TAG (Adult Games)

  • Always agree on consent level BEFORE starting
  • Use the pause button if things move too fast
  • Games are best with established connections, not strangers

3. TAGS Games: The Consent Protocol

Before Any Game

  1. Both players review the game description
  2. Agree on the consent level (🟢 🟡 🔴)
  3. Confirm neither has hard limits that conflict
  4. Establish a safe word or exit signal

During the Game

  • Check in verbally — "Still good?" goes a long way
  • Honor the agreed consent level — don't try to escalate
  • Use the pause button without guilt

After the Game

  • Debrief if needed — what worked, what didn't
  • Respect if the other person wants space
  • Never share or screenshot game sessions

4. Meeting in Person

First Meetings

  • Meet in public places initially
  • Tell a friend where you're going
  • Use your own transportation
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, leave

Using Tonight Mode

  • Only enable when you're genuinely available
  • Your exact location is never shared — only venue
  • Disable it when you're done for the night

After Meeting

  • Update the Nest with notes
  • Consider adding a Vouch if the person was genuine
  • Use Shredder gracefully if there's no connection

5. Vouch Chain: Building Trust

  • Only vouch for people you've actually met
  • Vouches are public — your reputation is attached
  • You can revoke a vouch if someone's behavior changes
  • More vouches = more visibility in Discover

6. When Things Go Wrong

If You Feel Unsafe

  • Block immediately — no explanation needed
  • Report the profile with details
  • If it's an emergency, contact local authorities

If Boundaries Are Crossed

  • Use the in-app report feature
  • Save any screenshots (messages, profile)
  • Our Safety Team reviews all reports within 24 hours

If You Made a Mistake

  • Apologize sincerely and directly
  • Accept if the other person needs space or wants to disconnect
  • Learn and do better next time

7. The Golden Rules

  1. Consent is ongoing — check in, always
  2. Honesty builds trust — be authentic in your profile and conversations
  3. Respect is non-negotiable — all identities, orientations, and bodies
  4. Communication prevents problems — when in doubt, talk it out
  5. Your safety comes first — trust your instincts
