Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Everloop ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our narrative platform and personal mythology builder.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, and profile details
  • Story Content: Characters, arcs, lore pages, and worldbuilding you create
  • Reflection Data: Daily/weekly loop responses and journal entries
  • Memory Integration: Real-life memories you choose to incorporate

2.2 Automatically Collected

  • Usage patterns and feature engagement
  • Device and browser information
  • Analytics for service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize your Everloop experience
  • Power AI-driven story suggestions and creative tools
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Send relevant updates and communications

4. Your Story, Your Data

Your creative content belongs to you. We provide tools for you to export, backup, and delete your stories and mythology at any time. We will never use your personal narratives for advertising or sell them to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your stories and personal information.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and export your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and content
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
